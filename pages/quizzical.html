Quizzical

Projekt Overview

"Quizzical" is a React app that can be used to test general knowledge at three different levels of difficulty. The questions are provided by the "Open Trivia Database" API. Those who prefer to put their knowledge in a specific area to the test can choose just one of the 24 subject areas.

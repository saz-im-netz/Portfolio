My Movie Watchlist

Projekt Overview

The "My Movie Watchlist" project consists of two pages: the movie search and the movie list. The movie title can be used to query movies from the "Open Movie Database", which are displayed in the movie search. These can then be saved on the movie list and accessed at any time.
